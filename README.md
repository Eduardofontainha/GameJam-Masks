Game: Fallen Mask

About

You control a survivor who falls into an ancient cave during an attack. Lost, wounded, and without answers, he finds a mysterious mask that grants him the power to defeat his enemies. Different weapons, magic, and abilities emerge, forcing the player to constantly adapt.

The game is a 2D action platformer focused on fast-paced combat, memorable bosses, and linear progression.

Controls

A / ← – Move Left

D / → – Move Right

↑ / Z – Jump

Mouse / Enter – Attack
